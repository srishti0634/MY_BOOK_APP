Magnify & Zoom

Drift and Luminous are two lightweight js libraries that are easy to implement on your site to enable zoom and magnification for your images.

  • Hover over the camera image to magnify!
  • You can also click to zoom!
  • Works as touch on mobile devices!
  • imgix makes it simple to dynamically size.
  • (Psst… try making your browser window smaller!)

Drift + Luminous Demo

This demo combines Drift's "zoom on hover" functionality with the Luminous lightbox. Move your mouse over the image (or touch it) to see Drift in action, then click to show Luminous!
